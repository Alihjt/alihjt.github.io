Ali Hojjat

ML Researcher | ML Engineer | Data Scientist

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Technical University of Hamburg (TUHH), specializing in efficient machine learning. My research focuses on developing lightweight vision models, including Vision Transformers (ViTs), vision-language models (VLMs), and video/image compression techniques optimized for edge devices. Previously, I worked as a Data Scientist at DIVAR, Iran's largest classifieds platform with over 60 million users, where I built high-performance ML systems at scale. My work has been presented at leading conferences such as NeurIPS, ACM MobiSys, and CVPRW. I also actively contribute to the research community as a reviewer for top-tier conferences, including ICML and NeurIPS.
